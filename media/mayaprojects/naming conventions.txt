Objects in maya -
	Actual object - 
		type_[optional:environmenttype]_size(# of lanes/something sensible)_[optional:turntype]_meshtype
		ex: road_city_fourlane_plane
	Material
		type__[optional:environmenttype]size(# of lanes/something sensible)_[optional:turntype]_materialtype
		ex: road_city_fourlane_blinn
		
Textures -
	type_[optional:environmenttype]_size(# of lanes/something sensible)_[optional:turntype]_"texture"
	ex: road_city_fourlane_texture
